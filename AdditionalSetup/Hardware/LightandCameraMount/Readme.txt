
These are the STL printable files for the Light mounts used in the setup to fix DeepRed LED lights.
M5 nuts and bolts are needed to assemble the full mount.

They description of files are as follows:

Mount_Arm_Straight_*.stl are the straight arm of 30mm and 60mm each and are used to extend length in each axis.

Axis_Change_Connector.stl are the mount attachment used to change the axis of the straight attachment as the same suggests.

Mount_Flat_*.stl are the end attachement used to fix LEDs at the mounts end.

Mount_Raspberry_Pi_CameraFRONT.stl is used to fix the raspberry pi camera v2 at the end.
